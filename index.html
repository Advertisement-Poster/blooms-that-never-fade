How It's Made

A peek behind the petals — every bloom is a labour of love.

Step 01

Colour Selection

We hand-pick premium pipe cleaners in the perfect palette for each design, ensuring vibrant, long-lasting colours.

Step 02

Shaping Petals

Each petal is individually twisted and shaped to mimic the natural curves and textures of real flowers.

Step 03

Assembly

Petals, stems, and leaves are carefully combined, adjusting each angle until the bloom looks just right.

Step 04

Final Touches

Details like curled edges, leaf veins, and subtle colour blends bring each piece to life before wrapping.
